About Us

Welcome to Water Compass, your number one source for all your water needs. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of services, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2018 by Shobhit Goyal, Water Compass has come a long way from its beginnings in a Copenhagen. When Shobhit Goyal first started out, his passion for helping other people be more eco-friendly, providing the best water for his fellow people drove him to do intense research, quit his day job, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the India, and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the water industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
